HIGH SECURITY PRINTING

HIGH SECURITY PRINTING

Colour Stop is a certified high-security printer, recognized by Intergraf and ISO 14298*. We employ trusted graphic design software to incorporate advanced security elements into our compositions, ensuring document integrity. 

we assemble, laminate, and shape card layers efficiently. Our commitment to security extends to the application of diffractive elements and innovative, in-house developed security features.

DESIGN

At Colour Stop, our studio boasts exceptionally talented graphic designers who excel at enhancing existing designs or crafting entirely new compositions from scratch.

Recognizable for their blend of elegance and security, Colour Stop designs stand out with their unique aesthetic appeal and robust security features.

SMARTCARDS

Colour Stop is honored to be among the select few high-security printers globally, certified by prestigious organizations including ISO for quality, security, and environmental standards, as well as payment card issuers and international security standards.

 Annually, Colour Stop manufactures approximately one billion prepaid phone cards and 300 million smart cards, utilizing various materials such as polycarbonate, PVC, and cellulose. Our automated finishing lines ensure precise assembly of smart cards, integration of electronics, and protection of sensitive information.

PASSPORT DATAPAGE

Colour Stop has expanded its offerings to include supplying polycarbonate datapages to passport manufacturers and national printing offices. 

Our patented technology is specifically designed for effortless insertion into booklets while maintaining robust resistance against counterfeiting attempts.

E-STICKERS

Colour Stop is at the forefront of producing UHF e-Stickers, offering cutting-edge technology for secure and efficient long-range windshield tags.

With versatility spanning multiple applications including electronic tolling, gate access, vehicle identification, and fleet management systems, our products redefine convenience and reliability in the modern automotive landscape.

PRODUCTION OF THE CARRIERS

In 2013, we invested in a manufacturing line dedicated to the production of card carriers. This workshop integrates printing, cutting and finishing equipment.

Capable of manufacturing all types of POS materials. We now have the capacity to design all types of marketing materials, including the most complex: pockets, cases, booklets.

CONNECTED FACTORY

Colour Stop has implemented an automated information system (MES) for online monitoring of production steps. Leveraging IoT technology, each equipment is connected to a central management system, 

enabling real-time performance measurement, identification of production break causes, and anticipation of maintenance interventions. Analysis of these indicators fosters continuous improvement.

QUALITY AND RESPECT FOR THE ENVIRONMENT

Colour Stop is known for its top-quality products and services, reflecting our total commitment to excellence. Our production process includes rigorous quality control through automatic measurements, systematic sampling, and random sampling. Additionally, we prioritize environmental responsibility by responsibly disposing of production waste and promoting material recycling.
